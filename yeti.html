Yeti $179.99/100g

Yetis are large ape-like creatures, standing about 3.5 meters in height, and are covered in thick white fur. They have jaws filled with teeth resembling human teeth, but with larger and more defined canines. They have immense strength, and are fast enough to catch up to bears and even wolfs. They live high up on mountains where there is lots of snow to camouflage themselves in.

Meat description: Yeti meat is tough and has a rough texture. The closest comparison would be that of bear meat. But anyone who eats the meat will increase their hair growth by a significant amount. It will even grow back a full head of hair, permanently.
Reccomended demographic: Bald/balding people and cancer patients.
